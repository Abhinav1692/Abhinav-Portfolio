IIIT, NAGPUR
(Engineering College in Nagpur)

Hello, I am going to tell you about my college IIIT, Nagpur. Indian Institute of Information Technology, Nagpur is one of the centrally funded technical institutes located in Nagpur. It was established in 2016. Being a student of this college, I will share all about this college that you should know if you want to take admission in it.

Campus

Since college is in the development stage, only the first phase of the construction is completed, which included Academic Block, Residential Block, Admin Block, Hostel. The government has allotted 100 acres of the plot for the campus. It has separate playgrounds for football, cricket, volleyball, basketball. On the campus, cleanliness is taken care of and maintained in every aspect. Green plants were also planted to make it environment-friendly and beautiful.

Academics

Prof.AG Kothari is the dean academic of the institute. The senate of the institute ( constitutes of director, dean, Tcs head, Nagpur, hods)makes and amends the rules of academic activities. The institute offers two branches in B.Tech namely CSE AND ECE. To get into this college, you need to clear JEE MAINS and have at least 97 percentile for the general category. you can explore the cutoff of previous years here. Each semester has two sessional exams and one end-semester exam. The syllabus of both branches is up to date according to the current market scenario. Professors are highly talented and very supportive.

Facilities
Mess & Hostel

Girls and Boys have separate hostels but have the same mess. Hostel rooms are fully air-conditioned and are available as 2-seater,3-seater, 4-seater. The mess has the capacity of 500 students at a time. cleanliness and hygiene are taken care of and maintained in every aspect.

Sports & Gym

The institute provides facilities for both indoor games and outdoor games. it has a big playground for cricket and football and has separate courts for volleyball, badminton. Every year national-level event "Inter IIIT Sports" meet organizes by the IIIT'S in which students of every IIIT college participate and get exposure. It has a special room for the GYM which has all the types of equipment. There is no dedicated Gym trainer but have a sports teacher who used to guide us.

Extra Curricular Activities

The institute has two national level events (Technical event and Cultural event)named TANTRA FIESTA and ABHIVYAKTI. These events are organized by the students by getting sponsorship from different companies. During each event, different teams were made like Event management, Hospitality, and many more to take care of all the stuff.

Trianing & Placement Cell

This is the most important part of every engineering college.T&P Cell of this institute is very active and has good networks with the hiring managers of MNCs and unicorn Startups. In recent years , this institute has seen a rapid increment in placements. This year despite the corona pandemic, 80-85 % of students got placed in good companies with the highest package of 44.5 lpa. To know more, you can visit iiit, nagpur.
